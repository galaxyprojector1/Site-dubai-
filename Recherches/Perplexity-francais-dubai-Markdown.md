# Tarifs de formation de société à Dubaï (exemples français)

Créer une société à Dubaï attire de nombreux entrepreneurs français en raison d’avantages fiscaux et administratifs. Les coûts varient selon le type de société (continentale, freezone, offshore), le nombre de visas, et les services annexes. Voici une synthèse des tarifs actualisés, basée sur les offres de cabinets francophones spécialisés.

## 1. Exemples de packs et coûts chez des prestataires français

| Type de société | Coût total la 1ʳᵉ année | Inclus/Commentaires |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Société continentale (Mainland) | À partir de 9 500 €[^1_1] | Licence (~3 500 €), MoA/carte immigration (~800 €), bureau virtuel (~1 800 €), visa investisseur (~950 €), services d’installation à partir de 2 500 €[^1_1] |
| Freezone (zone franche) | 6 000 à 26 000 AED/an<br>(env. 1 400 à 6 500 €) [^1_2][^1_3] | Varie selon le quota de visas :<br>0 visa (1 400–4 000 €)<br>1 visa (1 900–4 900 €)<br>3 visas (3 700–6 474 €)<br>Ajout establishment card, e-channel et garanties[^1_2] |
| Offshore | À partir de 12 000 AED pour la création<br>(env. 3 000 €), puis 8 000–12 000 AED/an [^1_4][^1_5] | Création à distance, formation sur obligations légales, assistance compte bancaire, outils de facturation (selon le prestataire)[^1_5] |

## 2. Détail des principaux frais

- **Frais de licence commerciale**
    - Mainland : à partir de 15 000 AED (env. 3 700 €)[^1_3]
    - Freezone : à partir de 10 000 AED (env. 2 500 €)[^1_3]
- **Frais d’enregistrement** : généralement 3 000 à 5 000 AED (env. 740–1 230 €)[^1_3]
- **Approbation du nom commercial** : 500 à 1 000 AED (env. 125–250 €)[^1_3]
- **Visa investisseur** : 4 500 à 5 000 AED par personne (env. 1 230 €)[^1_3][^1_2]
- **Frais annexes** : establishment card (env. 2 000 AED, soit 500 €), e-channel (1 250–3 000 AED, soit 298–750 €), caution remboursable de 5 000 AED[^1_2]


## 3. Ce que comprennent certains packs français

- Assistance complète à la création
- Accompagnement banque et TVA
- Formation réglementaire de base
- Outils de facturation et comptabilité
- Création de logo, documents administratifs
- Consultation juridique initiale gratuite avec simulation chiffrée[^1_2][^1_5]


## 4. Points importants

- Les coûts annuels de renouvellement (licence, establishment card, bureau) sont à prévoir dès la 2ᵉ année.
- Chaque projet (activité, nombre de visés, location, structure, situation familiale) nécessite une simulation individualisée par le prestataire.
- Les tarifs varient selon : nature d’activité, zone d’implantation, nombre de collaborateurs/visas.

# Copywriting \& Landing Page Services in Dubai for French-Speaking Business Clients

Businesses in Dubai seeking to attract a French-speaking clientele have access to specialized copywriting and landing page design services. These services are provided by agencies and freelancers experienced in engaging international audiences, with many able to localize content and understand the nuances of the French market.

## Key Offerings

### 1. Copywriting Services

- **Types of Content:** Website copy, landing pages, product descriptions, blog articles, social media posts, emails, and ads.
- **SEO Integration:** Most agencies implement SEO best practices to ensure your business is visible in both French and Arabic/English search engines[^2_1][^2_2].
- **Localization:** Many Dubai-based agencies and freelancers offer copywriting in French, ensuring messages are tailored to resonate with French-speaking clients[^2_1][^2_2].
- **Creative \& Technical Writing:** Services range from creative storytelling (branding, slogans, emotional engagement) to technical copywriting for complex products and services[^2_2][^2_3].
- **Industry Adaptability:** Agencies support a diverse array of sectors, including e-commerce, tech, finance, hospitality, and legal services[^2_2].


### 2. Landing Page Design \& Optimization

- **Multilingual Capabilities:** Specialized agencies design and develop landing pages in French to maximize engagement and conversions from French-speaking audiences[^2_4][^2_2].
- **Conversion Focused:** Best practices include clear calls-to-action, minimal designs, responsive layouts, and ongoing optimization for better ROI[^2_4][^2_5].
- **Integrated Copy and Design:** Many providers offer bundled services — combining persuasive copywriting and compelling design for a seamless user experience[^2_4][^2_6][^2_3].
- **Analytics \& A/B Testing:** Professional agencies conduct testing and track key metrics to optimize content performance and conversion rates[^2_5].


## Selecting a Service Provider

### Leading Copywriting Agencies and Platforms in Dubai

| Agency/Platform | Services Provided | French Language Capability | Typical Budget |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |

| Freelance Marketplaces (Upwork, Malt)[^2_7][^2_8] | Direct access to French-speaking copywriters | Yes | Flexible |

### What to Ask When Choosing a Provider

- Do they have verified experience with French-speaking clients or in your sector?
- Can they provide samples of landing pages or copy in French?
- Do they offer both copywriting and design, or do you need to coordinate between providers?
- What is the process for revisions, localization, and integrating feedback?


## How These Services Benefit French-Speaking Businesses

- **Increased Conversions:** Localized copy and landing pages drive higher engagement and sales by addressing the linguistic and cultural preferences of French-speaking customers[^2_4][^2_2].
- **Brand Differentiation:** Professional storytelling and clear messaging position your business as the go-to partner for French expatriates or companies in Dubai[^2_2][^2_9].
- **SEO Visibility:** Well-optimized French-language content improves search rankings in relevant regions, driving organic traffic[^2_1][^2_2][^2_9].
- **Trust \& Professionalism:** French clients value clear, localized, and culturally relevant communication, helping to build trust and repeat business.


## How to Get Started

- Reach out to Dubai-based agencies with a strong track record in French copy and landing page work (see agencies above).
- For flexibility or tighter budgets, browse profiles on Malt or Upwork for French-native freelancers in Dubai[^2_7][^2_8].
- Request a portfolio or references specific to French-language work.
- Clarify the scope: translations, original copy, full-site work, landing page optimization, analytics/tracking, etc.

**Summary:**
Dubai offers a robust network of agencies and freelancers who provide copywriting and landing page services tailored for French-speaking business clients. Agencies such as Emirates Graphic, créo, and SOUTHNEXT, as well as freelance marketplaces like Upwork and Malt, offer both high-end and flexible solutions for engaging the French business community with compelling, conversion-oriented content and design[^2_4][^2_1][^2_2][^2_9][^2_3].
